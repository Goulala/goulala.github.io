Brain Disease Spatial Omics Laboratory

We study how neural circuits and molecular cell states support learning, memory, and decision-making, and how these processes are disrupted in brain disease.

Research

Spatial omics of brain disease

We develop and apply spatial omics approaches to map molecular and cellular changes in healthy and diseased brain states.

Neural circuits of memory and decision-making

We investigate how neural circuits encode experience, guide behavior, and become maladaptive in neurological and psychiatric disorders.

Multimodal brain mapping

We combine spatial transcriptomics, circuit neuroscience, imaging, and computational analysis to understand brain function across scales.
