Zhou Lab

Spatial omics, neural circuits, and molecular mechanisms of brain disorders.

Mapping brain function across molecules, cells, synapses, and circuits.

We develop and apply spatial technologies to study learning, memory, decision-making, and disease-associated changes in the brain.

The lab is based at the Interdisciplinary Research Center on Biology and Chemistry, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Shanghai.

Welcome

The Zhou Lab studies how molecular cell states and neural circuits support behavior, and how these systems are disrupted in neurological and psychiatric disorders.
