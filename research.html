Develop Novel Spatial Mapping Technologies

We build on imaging-based in situ sequencing because it preserves rich spatial information within intact tissue. Our goal is to expand this cutting-edge technology by incorporating additional modalities, enabling us to decipher brain features beyond molecular identity alone.

Engram Network of Memory and Decision-making

We investigate how engram cells form network and encode experience, guide behavior, and become maladaptive in neurological and psychiatric disorders.

Multimodal brain mapping

We combine spatial transcriptomics, circuit neuroscience, imaging, and computational analysis to understand brain function across scales.
