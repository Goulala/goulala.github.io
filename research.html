Research

Spatial and circuit-level approaches to brain function and disease.

Spatial omics of brain disorders

We map molecular and cellular changes in healthy and diseased brain states using spatial omics approaches.

Neural circuits of memory and decision-making

We investigate how neural circuits encode experience, guide behavior, and become maladaptive in neurological and psychiatric disorders.

Multimodal brain mapping

We combine spatial transcriptomics, circuit neuroscience, imaging, and computational analysis to understand brain function across scales.
